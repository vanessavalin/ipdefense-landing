About IP Defense

What is IP Defense?

IP Defense is an operational brand enforcement service for mobile app companies. We identify copycat apps, impersonation accounts, infringing listings, and brand abuse across app stores, social platforms, and the web — then manage the entire process of getting them removed or remediated. We are not a law firm and do not provide legal advice.

Is IP Defense a law firm?

No. IP Defense is an operational enforcement service, not a law firm. We do not provide legal advice or legal representation. We specialise in the operational side of brand protection: identifying violations, gathering evidence, preparing platform complaints, managing escalations, and tracking cases to resolution.

How is IP Defense different from hiring an IP lawyer?

IP lawyers are the right choice for formal legal action: cease and desist letters, litigation, trademark prosecution. IP Defense operates before that threshold — operational enforcement work that platforms respond to directly. This is typically faster and less expensive than legal action for most app brand abuse cases.

What kinds of companies does IP Defense work with?

We work primarily with mobile app publishers, app portfolio operators, AI app companies, and creator app platforms — any company where brand integrity drives real revenue.

Understanding App Brand Abuse

What is a copycat app?

A copycat app is an application published by an unauthorised third party that imitates your app's name, icon, screenshots, description, or core functionality to divert users searching for your app.

How common is app brand abuse?

Extremely common for any app with meaningful brand recognition. Our experience across hundreds of cases shows most apps above a certain download threshold have at least one active copycat or infringing listing at any time.

What is the difference between a competing app and a copycat?

A competing app offers a similar product with its own distinct brand identity. A copycat specifically imitates your brand's identity or name to create confusion and benefit from your brand equity.

How do copycat apps affect my app's revenue?

Through several simultaneous mechanisms: intercepting organic search traffic, inflating customer acquisition cost on paid channels, contaminating category quality perception, and direct trust damage in impersonation cases.

What platforms do copycats typically operate on?

Apple App Store and Google Play are primary, but brand abuse often extends to Instagram, TikTok, Facebook, X, YouTube, and lookalike websites or domains.

What is the difference between impersonation and infringement?

Impersonation is pretending to be your company or app. Infringement is using your protected brand elements without authorisation. Most copycat situations involve both.

The IP Defense Service

What exactly does IP Defense do?

We manage brand enforcement end-to-end: Discover (scan for violations), Document (build evidence files), Resolve (submit complaints, manage escalations, track to resolution).

Which platforms do you enforce across?

Apple App Store, Google Play, Instagram, TikTok, X, YouTube, Facebook, and website/domain registrations.

How long does a typical case take to resolve?

Clear App Store violations typically resolve in two to four weeks. Google Play often takes three to five weeks. Social platform cases can be as fast as 48-72 hours. Complex cases can take six to twelve weeks.

What do you need from me to get started?

Your app's App Store and/or Google Play listing URL, basic brand information, and a way to reach you with questions. We handle the scanning, evidence gathering, and complaint preparation.

Can you guarantee that an infringing app will be removed?

No — platform enforcement decisions are made by Apple and Google, not us. What we guarantee is thorough documentation, correct categorisation, proper submission, active follow-up, and escalation when needed.

Results and Reporting

What outcomes can I realistically expect?

Based on our track record: app removals, name changes, logo/branding updates, impersonation account takedowns, and corrected misleading listings.

Will my app's search rankings improve after enforcement?

Typically yes. Once a competing copycat is removed, organic search performance usually recovers within one to three app store index cycles.

How will I know what IP Defense has found and resolved?

Regular case status updates throughout — when violations are found, complaints submitted, platforms respond, and cases close, with before/after evidence.

What happens if new copycats appear after my case is resolved?

Protection and Portfolio clients have ongoing monitoring included. Assessment clients would need a new engagement or upgrade to a retainer.

What happens if a platform does not remove an infringing app?

We escalate — resubmit with more evidence, use higher-level support channels, and pursue parallel enforcement on other platforms targeting the same party.
