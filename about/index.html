About

We protect apps the way operators think about the problem.

IP Defense is an operational enforcement service — not a law firm, not a monitoring tool. We find brand abuse, build the evidence, submit the complaints, and track every case to resolution.

App brand abuse follows a predictable pattern: a copycat registers a similar name, clones your icon, stuffs your keywords into their metadata, and starts diverting the traffic your product quality earned.

Platform enforcement works — when done correctly. The difference between a complaint that gets acted on and one that doesn't is knowing the right violation category, the right evidence format, and when to escalate. That operational expertise is what we provide.

We've run this process across the App Store, Google Play, Meta, TikTok, X, YouTube, and web registrars. 200+ resolved cases across 6+ platforms is the proof point.
