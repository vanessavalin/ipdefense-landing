The Threats

What's targeting your app's
growth right now

As apps scale, bad actors follow. These are the four threats app teams worry about most — and the ones we resolve.

Copycat Apps & Lookalike Listings

Near-identical apps, names, icons, and screenshots built to intercept your traffic. Every install they capture is a download — and a subscriber — taken from you.

Brand & Social Impersonation

Fake accounts and "official" profiles that scam your users under your name. You lose the revenue today — and the user's trust forever.

Keyword & Search Abuse

Bad actors stuffing and bidding on your branded keywords — taxing your ad spend and hijacking the searches that should convert to your app.

Misleading & Infringing Listings

App Store and Google Play violations that confuse your customers and dilute your rankings — and that platforms will act on when properly reported.

The Process

We handle the full
enforcement lifecycle

Your team stays focused on product and growth. We own the entire operational side of brand protection — from first discovery to final resolution.

Step 01

Discover

Step 02

Step 03
