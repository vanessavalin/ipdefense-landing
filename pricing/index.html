What affects your scope?

These are the factors that shape what your engagement looks like and what it costs:

Number of apps

Each additional app adds monitoring surface and potential violation volume. Portfolio operators typically see more efficient per-app pricing at scale.

Current violation volume

A new app with one known copycat is a different scope than an established app with 20 active infringing listings across six platforms.

Platform coverage required

App Store and Google Play only is narrower scope than a full sweep including Instagram, TikTok, X, YouTube, Facebook, and web domains.

One-time vs. ongoing

A single enforcement round cleans up what exists now. Ongoing monitoring and management prevents the problem from rebuilding.

Pricing FAQs

Do you offer a free assessment before I commit?

Yes — the free IP Risk Review is a no-commitment initial look at your app's brand exposure. We check your App Store and Google Play listing, run searches for similar apps, and review major social platforms. It is not the full systematic scan of the Assessment tier, but it gives you a meaningful picture of your current exposure before you decide on next steps.

Is there a minimum contract length?

No. Protection and Portfolio are month-to-month retainers. Assessment is a single engagement with no ongoing commitment. We earn continued business by delivering results.

Why don't you publish specific prices?

Because scope varies significantly. An app with one known copycat on one platform is a fundamentally different engagement from a portfolio of 15 apps with ongoing violation volumes across six platforms. Publishing a single number would either underprice complex engagements or overprice simple ones. A short conversation about your situation lets us give you an accurate number.

What if I start with Assessment and then want to move to Protection?

Straightforward. Assessment clients who want ongoing coverage can upgrade to a Protection or Portfolio retainer after their initial engagement. We will already have a complete picture of your brand's violation landscape from the Assessment, which makes the transition immediate rather than requiring a new scanning cycle.
